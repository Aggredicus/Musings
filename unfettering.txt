Stumbling in the dark
away from warmth of certainty
the child wanders.
In their fumbling
they sprint toward discovery
crevasse and pitfall untrodden.
Pining in the night
far from the warmth of babe
the mother weeps.
In her cessation
of closeness in heart
between her creation
and her dreams.
