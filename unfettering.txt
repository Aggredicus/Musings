Stumbling in the dark
away from warmth of certainty
each child wanders.
In their fumbling
each wants of discovery
while each crevasse
passess untrodden.
Pining in the night
far from the warmth of babe
each mother weeps.
In her cessation
of closeness in heart
between her creation
and her dreams.
